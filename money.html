​Purchasing ​Step 1 Research Every Component You Intend To Buy

Remember, for a pc to work everything will depend on your hardware working correctly. Look for good reviews for your product. Some well respected hardware reviewers are Linus Tech Tips, JayzTwoCents or Bitwit. Once you've found a decently reviewed component, look for negative reviews of the component. You may find that the component is great for certain uses, but terrible for your uses.

​Purchasing ​Step 2 Find Your Components

​1. Find a Processor
2. Find a Motherboard that fits your Processor
3. Find a cooler for your Processor that fit your Motherboard if necessary
4. Find RAM that is compatible with your Motherboard
5. Find a Hard Drive/SSD/ NVMe/M.2 which fit your memory needs
6. Find a Graphics Card if necessary
7. Find a Power Supply that handle the power load of the all the components
8. Find a Case that can fit all of your components

​Purchasing ​Step ​3 Know Where To Buy PC Components

​In store locations such as Best Buy will stock computer components, but you can find parts for cheaper if you shop online: Amazon, eBay, and NewEgg.
